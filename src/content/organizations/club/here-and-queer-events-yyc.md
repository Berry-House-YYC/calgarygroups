Here and Queer Events YYC has been organizing inclusive, community building 2SLGBTQIA+ gatherings in YYC for 17+ years. Events include queer camps hosted outside of Calgary. Pricing is dependent on the event, but is likely over $125 for 3 nights at a campsite. There is an option to pay in installments.
