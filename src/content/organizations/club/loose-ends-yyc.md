Loose Ends is a Calgary-based collective looking to bring people together over our shared love for creating, and building community in a safe space for all. Everyone is welcome to participate, no matter the craft or skill level you bring to the table. We aim to open the doors for anyone from any path, offer creative outlets for mediums you have yet to explore without the financial commitment. We host get-togethers and workshops at your favourite local spots, so if you're looking for a friend or something new to try, you're in the right place.
