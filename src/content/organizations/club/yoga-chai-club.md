Led by a certified yoga teacher, Yoga Chai Club hosts yoga each sunday at 10 AM at his studio in Hillhurst. Beginners are welcome. After the session, you can enjoy chai and socialize. Events are $15 each, including chai, however you can pay a 3-month membership at $150, totaling $12.50 per session.

The instructor is dedicated to accessibility for "a diverse range of individuals, including those with disabilities, individuals who have experienced trauma, individuals recovering from concussions, and those seeking professional development."
