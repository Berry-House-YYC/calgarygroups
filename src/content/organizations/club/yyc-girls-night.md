YYC Girls Night hosts paid events for women at various price points in collaboration with small businesses. They curate experiences across wellness, social events, business networking, and life-skills workshops, creating a community for women to grow personally, professionally, and socially. Most attendees are ages 20-40 but the club is open to 18+. Also hosts Calgary Girls Book Club.
