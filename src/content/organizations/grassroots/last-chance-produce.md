A grassroots mutual aid organization running weekly pay-what-you-can food markets to support low-income folks. They rely on food and community donations and volunteer support to operate. They are barrier-free in that they do not require identification. They also strive to be accessible, letting people with mobility aids in first to avoid fatigue from waiting in line.
