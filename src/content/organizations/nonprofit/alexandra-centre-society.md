The Alexandra Centre Society (ACS) cultivates a vibrant, inclusive, local community where everyone is welcome, and our historical legacy is celebrated. It serves the Calgary neighbourhoods of Inglewood, Ramsay, Beltline - Victoria Park, and East Village. As a prominent nonprofit in Calgary, they rent their space to other groups who host events like crafternoons, clothing swaps, and art markets.
