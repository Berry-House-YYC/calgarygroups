Youth racing team for Canoe/kayak, and adult recreation and racing teams for Outrigger canoe. Western Canada's largest flat water racing club, based on the Glenmore reservoir. The CCC supports all who are interested in racing boats and recreational paddling and has produced several national and international level athletes. Club programs aside from the racing team include general memberships to access recreational fleet of canoes and kayaks, full day summer camp options, year round after school programs, and physical education classes through local schools.
