The Calgary Modern Quilt Guild welcomes all who want to explore and create modern quilts. We are organized as a guild under the umbrella of the Modern Quilt Guild, an organization dedicated tp the growth and development of modern quilting.

The Calgary Modern Quilt guild meets monthly, either virtual or in person. Our meetings are about two hours, and feature a program component of education, discussion or display, followed by a business meeting of announcements and reminders, with show and tell to follow.
