Calgary Safety Connect (CSC) is focused on bringing quality professional development and educational opportunities to the local safety community. It is our goal to build a strong social network for safety professionals, provide great content, and encourage the sharing ideas in a relaxed informal setting, all the while keeping it affordable. If you’re just starting out in safety, or you’re a mid-career safety professional, or even if you’re a seasoned pro, Calgary Safety Connect has something for you!
