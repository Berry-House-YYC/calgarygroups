A volunteer-driven non-profit organization providing recreational, social, and educational programs to help seniors stay active and engaged. Located in NW Calgary, it offers a wide range of activities from fitness classes to arts and crafts, socials, classes, and discounted luncheons. Donations are requested for some events and programs.

Note that you do not have to be a senior to become a member! The annual membership fee is $40 for this organization, or $55 "Super Membership" unlocks access to the other 13 associated organizations:

- Bow Cliff Seniors
- Bowness Seniors
- Calgary 50+ Games
- Good Companions 50+
- Greater Forest Lawn 55+ Activity Centre
- Kensington Legion
- Ogden 50+ Activity Centre
- Parkdale Nifty 50s
- Rainbow Elders
- Seniors Acting Lab
- SW 55+ Connect
- Unison at Kerby Centre for the 55 Plus
- West Hillhurst Go-Getters
