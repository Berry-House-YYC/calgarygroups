Good Neighbour is a volunteer-run organization providing low-barrier support for unhoused and low-income individuals (clothing, food, toiletries). They co-host markets and other pay-what-you-can charity events, as well as advocate for accessible housing / urban issues and politics. They also run Good Thrift, a thrift store that helps fund this initiative.
