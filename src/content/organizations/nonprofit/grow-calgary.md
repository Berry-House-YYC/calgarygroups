"Grow Calgary is a community farm founded in 2011. We are dedicated to growing produce for individuals experiencing food insecurity, and providing small-scale farming education to people of all ages. Along with donating our produce, we advocate for system-level change to address food insecurity in our communities. We utilize agroecology farming practices and are a volunteer-run organization."
