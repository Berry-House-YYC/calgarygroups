Jam Club is a free after-school drop-in program for teens looking to practice their skills and explore their creative potential, all the while making new friends. All skill levels are welcome.

Jam Club is led by team of musicians and educators and is a part of the National Music Centre, a nonprofit museum operating within Studio Bell.
