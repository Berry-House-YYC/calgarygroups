MS Canada offers a variety of resources, events, and support options for people in Canada living with MS. They offer in-person and virtual peer support groups. Calgary Under 30 Coffee and Connect Group is a social group that meets once a month. Calgary Coffee Group hosts one virtual and one in-person meeting per month. Both groups are intended to be social in nature and do not always address the topic of MS.

To learn more and join, contact the MS Knowledge Network.
