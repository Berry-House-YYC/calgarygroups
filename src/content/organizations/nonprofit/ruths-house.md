Ruth's House supports families of African descent who have survived domestic violence. They have a women's shelter and a men's shelter, providing cultural food, translation support for service navigation, and therapy. They also offer in-home support. They have programs for children, youth, and adult women and men advocating for the prevention family violence and have peer groups for forming healthy social connections.
