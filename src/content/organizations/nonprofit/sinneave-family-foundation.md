The Sinneave Family Foundation offers resources related to the workplace, executive function, social skills, and emotional coping for Autistic youth and adults. They also offer online and in-person peer groups for Autistic individuals, as well as for caregivers. In-person and research-related activities are conducted at the Ability Hub and this organization is run by the University of Calgary.
