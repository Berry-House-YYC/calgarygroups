An organization dedicated to creating safe spaces for volleyball for the 2SLGBTQ+ community. Hosts drop-in, tournaments including the Western Cup. Indoor Volleyball, intermediate and competitive, is offered at a drop-in rate of $9, while Beach Volleyball events are for beginners, and appear to be available for free.
