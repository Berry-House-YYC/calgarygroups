Every Body Stronger offers fitness training, yoga, and physical therapies alongside events and other advocacy work. They aim to be size-inclusive, queer-friendly, and trauma-informed.

Hosts Queer Strength Club, a weekly pay-what-you-can drop-in space. Whether you're new to movement or an experienced pro, QSC is dedicated to helping folks move their bodies how they see fit. Our goal is to support folks in their movements so they feel confident and safe to engage in exercise.
