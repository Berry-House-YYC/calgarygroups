Raising Readers provides structured, evidence-based literacy tutoring for children who are struggling to learn to read. We support early readers and elementary-aged students using Science of Reading–aligned instruction, including phonemic awareness, phonics, decoding, fluency, and spelling.

Our services are designed for families seeking targeted, individualized reading support—particularly for children with reading difficulties or those who need a strong foundational approach. Before reaching out, families should know that tutoring is data-driven, structured, and requires consistent attendance and family partnership for best results.
