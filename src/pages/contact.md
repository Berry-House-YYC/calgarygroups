    <h2>Contact</h2>
    
    <h3>What to include</h3>

    <p>To help us respond quickly, please include:</p>

    <ul>
      <li>the organization name</li>
      <li>the page URL (or a screenshot)</li>
      <li>what needs changing and why</li>
      <li>a source link (official website, social profile, etc.)</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Corrections & updates</h3>

    <p>If you spot incorrect information, outdated links, or an organization that has changed status, email us and we'll update it.</p>

    <h3>Removal requests</h3>

    <p>If you represent an organization and want your listing updated or removed, email us from an address associated with the organization (or include a public verification link).</p>

    <h3>Submitting a new organization</h3>

    <p>Use the submission form on <a href="/submit/">/submit/</a> (preferred). You can also email the details.</p>

    <h2>Email</h2>

    <p><strong>General inquiries and submissions:</strong><br>
    <a href="mailto:info@berryhouse.ca">info@berryhouse.ca</a></p>

    <p><strong>Technical issues or bug reports:</strong><br>
    <a href="mailto:tech@berryhouse.ca">tech@berryhouse.ca</a></p>

    <p>We typically respond within 2-3 business days.</p>

    <h2>Other ways to contribute</h2>

    <ul>
      <li><strong>Report issues on <a href="https://github.com/Berry-House-YYC/calgarygroups/issues" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">GitHub</a></strong> - for technical problems or feature requests</li>
      <li><strong>Submit a pull request</strong> - if you're comfortable editing Markdown files directly</li>
      <li><strong>Share the directory</strong> - help more Calgarians discover these community resources!</li>
    </ul>
