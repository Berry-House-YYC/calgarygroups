## Contact

Email: [calgary@berryhouse.ca](mailto:calgary@berryhouse.ca)

## What to include

To help us respond quickly, please include:

- the organization name
- the page URL (or a screenshot)
- what needs changing and why
- a source link (official website, social profile, etc.)

## Corrections & updates

If you spot incorrect information, outdated links, or an organization that has changed status, email us and we’ll update it.

## Removal requests

If you represent an organization and want your listing updated or removed, email us from an address associated with the organization (or include a public verification link).

## Submitting a new organization

Use the submission form on [/submit/](/submit/) (preferred). You can also email the details.

## Privacy

Please don’t send private phone numbers, home addresses, or other sensitive information. We only publish public contact details.

## Response time

We’re a small team. We usually reply within a few days, depending on volume.

## GitHub

If you’re comfortable with GitHub, you can also:

- report an issue: https://github.com/Berry-House-YYC/calgarygroups/issues
- submit a pull request with an edit to `src/content/organizations/**`
