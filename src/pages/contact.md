<div class="prose prose-slate max-w-none dark:prose-invert">
    <p>Have a question about Calgary Groups? Want to update an organization listing, or share information about a community group we should feature? We're here to help and would love to hear from you!</p>
    
    <h2>How to reach us</h2>
    
    <h3>What to include in your message</h3>

    <p>To help us respond quickly and make sure your message gets to the right place, please include:</p>

    <ul>
      <li>the organization name</li>
      <li>the page URL (or a screenshot)</li>
      <li>what needs changing and why</li>
      <li>a source link (official website, social profile, etc.)</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Updates and corrections</h3>

    <p>Keeping our directory accurate is a community effort! If you notice outdated information, broken links, or details that have changed, please let us know. We review all submissions and update listings regularly.</p>

    <h3>Removing or updating listings</h3>

    <p>We respect the wishes of all organizations in our directory. If you represent an organization and would like to update your information or remove your listing, please email us from an official organization email address, or include a link to your organization's website/ social media so we can verify your request.</p>

    <h3>Sharing a new organization</h3>

    <p>Know of a Calgary-based group, club, or organization that should be in our directory? We're always looking to add new community resources! The easiest way is to use our <a href="/submit/">submission form</a>, but you're also welcome to email us the details directly.</p>

    <h2>Email us</h2>

    <p><strong>For general questions and submissions:</strong><br>
    <a href="mailto:info@berryhouse.ca">info@berryhouse.ca</a></p>

    <p><strong>For technical issues or bug reports:</strong><br>
    <a href="mailto:tech@berryhouse.ca">tech@berryhouse.ca</a></p>

    <p>We're a small team passionate about connecting Calgarians with their communities, so we appreciate your patience. We typically respond within 2-3 business days.</p>

    <h2>Other ways to contribute</h2>

    <p>Calgary Groups is a community project, and there are many ways to help make it better:</p>

    <ul>
      <li><strong>Report an issue on <a href="https://github.com/Berry-House-YYC/calgarygroups/issues" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">GitHub</a></strong> - spotted a technical problem or have a feature suggestion? Let us know!</li>
      <li><strong>Submit a pull request</strong> - if you're comfortable with Markdown and Git, you can help keep listings up to date</li>
      <li><strong>Share the directory</strong> - the more people who know about Calgary Groups, the more connected our community becomes!</li>
    </ul>
